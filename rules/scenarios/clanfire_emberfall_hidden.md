# Hidden Scenario Module: Clanfire: Emberfall

This module is for AI Custodian prompts or private Custodian notes. Do not show it to players. It assumes the core rules and Clanfire skin are already loaded.

## Scenario in one line
The clan's fire is dying, the hunt must succeed, and hungry things in the birch-line are already claiming the edge of the light.

## Default PCs
- Grak (Hunter): MGT 12 | FLT 11 | CUN 10 | SPR 8 | INS 8/8 | STM 7/7.
- Tarra (Shaman): MGT 6 | FLT 8 | CUN 12 | SPR 14 | INS 11/11 | STM 3/3.

## Truth: pick one twist
1. Rival hunters shadow the same quarry and will steal the kill if PCs hesitate.
2. Spirits are restless: prey is marked and demands a taboo cost to take.
3. Strangers watch from the ridge and leave unnaturally clean footprints.

Reveal the truth through signs, rather than exposition.

## Pressure guidance: Shadow
- Mark +1 Shadow for taboo breach, failed risky rite, bargaining with spirits, loud violence near the cave, ignoring a clear omen, or taking safety at someone else's cost.
- Purge Shadow through sacrifice, dangerous ritual, great hunt, story quest, or hard-won clan safety.
- Do not mark Shadow for every failed roll. Use it when failure touches taboo, spirits, fear, or survival.

## Optional clocks
- Hunger (0/6): tick on time passing, food lost, or empty return. Full means forced migration or starvation.
- Storm (0/6): tick on long exposure, loud action, or delay in the open. Full means whiteout; risky tests cost 1 Instinct bead or +1 Shadow.
- Pack Learns (0/4): tick when wolves smell blood, spot weakness, or find an easy path. Full means the pack surrounds the cave at night.

## Threat: Cave Wolf
- Soldier-tier 10: Attack FLT 10, defend FLT 10, STM 4, soak 0, edge +1 bite.
- Hook: if the wolf wins with margin 4 or more, it drags the target a few metres toward darkness.
- Tougher option: Elite 12, STM 5.
- Behaviour: wants food and advantage, not a fair fight. It tests, circles, snaps, retreats if badly hurt, and returns if it learns.

## Opening beat
Start with embers low, frost at the cave mouth, a teeth-on-bone crunch beyond the birches, and a slow hungry exhale.

Offer options:
1. Read tracks downwind (CUN).
2. Stalk the sound (FLT).
3. Feed fire and call spirits (SPR; fail = +1 Shadow).
4. Wake the clan and bar the cave (no roll; time passes).

Default fail costs: +1 Shadow, tick a clock, lose time or position, Stamina damage, damaged gear, or a harder truth revealed.

## Ending prompt
End on one unanswered question: What is making the birch-line wrong?

Offer next-session options:
1. Follow the wolf's blood toward the frozen stream (CUN).
2. Set a trap line and retreat before the storm turns (CUN).
3. Perform a fire-circle omen and pay its cost (SPR; fail = +1 Shadow).
4. Wake the clan and migrate before dawn (no roll; tick Hunger and Storm).

## Running stance
- Do not roll by default.
- Roll only when uncertainty and stakes both matter.
- State stakes before rolling.
- Use clocks and Shadow as costs instead of constant checks.
- Let combat end with retreat, fear, bargains, stolen meat, or changed position.
